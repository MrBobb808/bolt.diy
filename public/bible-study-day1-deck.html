Reason to Trust the Bible · #1

Manuscript Reliability

  • 5,800+ Greek New Testament manuscripts Manuscript: a handwritten copy of a document from before the printing press.
  • Earliest fragments are ~30 years from the original.
  • If we reject the Bible as unreliable, we'd have to throw out basically all of ancient history.
Reason to Trust the Bible · #2

The Dead Sea Scrolls

  • Discovered in 1947, dated back to 200 B.C.
  • Contained the Isaiah scroll — nearly identical to today's Bible.
  • 900+ ancient texts, including copies of the Hebrew Bible.
  • Confirms careful transmission of Scripture over centuries.
  • Proves the incredible preservation of Scripture.
Reason to Trust the Bible · #3

Prophetic Fulfillment

  • 300+ Messianic prophecies fulfilled by Jesus:
  • Birth — Micah 5:2
  • Life & ministry — Isaiah 61, Psalm 78
  • Death — Isaiah 53, Psalm 22

Discernment

Scripture vs. Personal Experience

  • Experiences are real.
  • Experiences are not authoritative.
  • Scripture interprets experience — not the other way around.
How To Study The Bible · Part 1

Read & Observe

  • 1. Read in ContextRead the chapter, not just the verse. Ask: Who's speaking? To whom? Why now?
  • 2. Observation Before InterpretationWhat does the text say? What words are repeated? What stands out?
  • 3. Scripture-to-Scripture MethodLet the Bible explain the Bible. Use cross-references. Compare OT foundations with NT fulfillment.
How To Study The Bible · Part 2

Context & Questions

  • 1. Historical & Cultural AwarenessThe Bible was written for us, not to us. Identify audience, covenant, and setting.
  • 2. Word StudyFocus on keywords. Use a concordance. Compare how the same word is used elsewhere.
  • 3. Ask the Right QuestionsWhat does this teach about God? About humanity? What response does it demand?
How To Study The Bible · Part 3

Pray & Stay Consistent

  • 1. Pray Before, During, and AfterAsk for illumination, not new revelation. Pray for humility, not confirmation.
  • 2. Be Consistent, Not ExtremeSmall, regular study beats random deep dives. One passage studied well > ten chapters skimmed.

✦ End of Day 1 — The Word is reliable. Now go study it.
